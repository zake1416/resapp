Role: IT project manager

About the job
Support Agile software development teams by coordinating sprint ceremonies, release activities, and production changes to ensure smooth and predictable delivery. Assist with ServiceNow change requests, CAB documentation, release planning, and Go/No-Go meetings while maintaining sprint artifacts and documentation. Provide administrative and operational support to cross-functional teams, including Project Managers and Engineers, by preparing reports, dashboards, and meeting materials. This role thrives in a fast-paced environment and is ideal for someone organized, detail-oriented, and eager to grow in Agile delivery practices.


Required Skills & Experience

 • 1–3 years of experience in project coordination, administration, or IT/software support
 • Exposure to Agile methodologies and sprint ceremonies
 • Experience with tools such as Jira, Confluence, ServiceNow, or similar platforms
 • Strong organizational, time management, and communication skills
 • Ability to create reports, dashboards, presentations, and meeting documentation
 • Proficiency in Microsoft Office or Google Workspace
 • Experience working in fast-paced, cross-functional technical environments
 • Familiarity with AI productivity tools (e.g., Microsoft Copilot, ChatGPT, Claude)
