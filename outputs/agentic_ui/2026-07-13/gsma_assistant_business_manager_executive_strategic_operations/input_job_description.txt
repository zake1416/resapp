About The Team

The CEO/COO Office team facilitates executive coordination, operational priorities, international programs, and high-level engagement activities throughout GSMA. This team collaborates closely with senior leadership, internal departments, external stakeholders, and international partners to advance operational excellence, promote executive alignment, and ensure the effective delivery of key organizational objectives.

As part of this dynamic and high-profile function, you will work directly with the Executive Business Manager to support executive operations, CEO/COO internal meetings, Climate & Sustainability reporting, and major event initiatives within the GSMA ecosystem.

About The Role

The Assistant Business Manager provides operational, administrative, and project support across executive priorities and workstreams. Reporting to the Executive Business Manager, the role includes calendar, travel, expense, meeting, stakeholder, and project coordination, as well as support for confidential and high-priority activity within the CEO/COO Office.

In addition, a key part of the role is supporting the planning and delivery of CEO/COO internal meetings. This includes agendas, executive communications, timelines, presentations, attendee coordination, logistics, travel, AV, briefing materials, and follow-up, helping ensure meetings run smoothly and professionally.

The role also supports delivery of GSMA’s flagship events, including MWC Barcelona, MWC Shanghai, M360, Nova, and selected GSMA Services initiatives. Responsibilities include timelines, stakeholder and vendor coordination, logistics, reporting, presentations, and support for other events or services as needed.

The role also supports projects such as the GSMA’s Climate & Sustainability programme, including sustainability tracking, stakeholder coordination, environmental reporting, and CDP support. This involves data gathering, timelines, documentation, and compliance activity aligned to GSMA’s sustainability goals.

For CDP reporting, the Assistant Business Manager will work with GSMA’s external sustainability partner, to support data collection, coordination, timelines, documentation, and related reporting deliverables.

The Assistant Project Manager will support process improvements, contribute to strategic projects, and manage smaller workstreams, with increasing ownership over time.

About You

This is an exciting opportunity for someone who is organised, proactive, and keen to build experience in a fast-paced global environment. You’ll work across international teams, gain exposure to senior leadership activity, and contribute to projects with visibility across the organisation.

In your first year, you’ll build strong foundations in project coordination, executive support, global event delivery, and sustainability reporting, with support from the team as you grow in confidence and responsibility.

We’re looking for someone with strong organisational skills, a positive and collaborative approach, and an interest in building a career in project management, operations, events, or executive support. You do not need to have done everything before; what matters most is a willingness to learn, manage multiple tasks, and work with care and professionalism.

You’ll develop communication and stakeholder management skills by working with teams across the organisation. The role also offers hands-on experience supporting meetings, travel, expenses, event coordination, and day-to-day project activity.

Some previous experience in coordination, administration, project support, events, or operations would be helpful, but we also welcome transferable skills and potential. Exposure to sustainability reporting or large-scale events is a plus, but not essential.

In return, you’ll gain broad exposure to GSMA initiatives and events, while building experience in project management, stakeholder engagement, sustainability, and executive operations in a supportive team environment.
